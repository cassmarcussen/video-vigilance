Video Vigilance

Grow your business reliably with YouTube Ads

Before submitting your video advertisement campaign to Google for review, run your video ad with our platform and receive feedback about how safe and accessible your video ad is.

Overview

Using machine learning models and APIs, Video Vigilance can give advertisers feedback on their video ad campaigns. We score the perceived impact their video ads may have on those who view their ads. Our results can help advertisers better understand the content they are putting out online and promotes transparency between YouTube Ads and advertisers.
